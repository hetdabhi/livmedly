Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book an appointment?

Go to the 'Appointments' page, choose your doctor, select a time slot, and confirm your booking.

2. How can I get my test results?

Login to your account, navigate to 'My Reports', and find your test results there.

3. Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, go to 'My Appointments' and modify or cancel your appointment at least 24 hours in advance.
